Three webhosting sites and a little about them

Hostinger is the first webhosting site I decided to look at. Hostinger offers a variety to different packages that include different services. Their most popular package costs six dollars a month. With that package from Hostinger you can use them to host up to 100 different websites, access to 100 GB of SSD storage, along with also providing GIT and SSH access which a programs to help with website design.

Visit Hostinger hostinger.com

Bluehost is another webhosting site, their services cost $2.95/month. Bluehost powers over 2 million websites worldwide. With Bluehost you recieve a free domain name for a year, an SSL certificate, and 24/7 support.

Visit Bluehost bluehost.com

The last webhosting site I'll look at is Go Daddy. Go daddy also has a variety to packages ranging from $2.99/month to $12.99/month. With go daddy you recieve both a free microsoft 365 email and a free domain. Go daddy also has both awardwinning 24/7 support and security monitoring. Go daddy also provides a 1-click install of over 125 applications to help with designing your website. I chose Go Daddy as my top host because of all the features they provide and then not costing much more than the other services.

Visit Go Daddy godaddy.com
